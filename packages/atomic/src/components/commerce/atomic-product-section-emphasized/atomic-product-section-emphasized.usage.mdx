## Usage

The `atomic-product-section-emphasized` component defines the emphasized area within a product template. Use it to highlight important product information that should stand out visually, such as pricing or promotions.

```html
<atomic-product-template>
  <template>
    <atomic-product-section-emphasized>
      <atomic-product-price></atomic-product-price>
    </atomic-product-section-emphasized>
  </template>
</atomic-product-template>
```
