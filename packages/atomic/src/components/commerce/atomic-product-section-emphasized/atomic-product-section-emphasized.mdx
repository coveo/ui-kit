## Usage
This section is intended to display the field that's important for its search criteria.
For example, in Commerce, a product's price is often more important than the title itself and needs to stand out prominently.

**Key Features:**
- Has a very large font size to draw attention to critical information
- Is the second closest element beneath the name section in the layout hierarchy
- Typically used for product pricing, but can contain any emphasized information
- Automatically hides when empty (no child content)

### Example within a Product Template
```html
<atomic-commerce-product-list>
  <atomic-product-template>
    <template>
      <atomic-product-section-visual>
        <atomic-product-image field="ec_thumbnails"></atomic-product-image>
      </atomic-product-section-visual>

      <atomic-product-section-name>
        <atomic-product-link class="font-bold"></atomic-product-link>
      </atomic-product-section-name>

      <atomic-product-section-emphasized>
        <atomic-product-price currency="USD"></atomic-product-price>
      </atomic-product-section-emphasized>

      <atomic-product-section-description>
        <atomic-product-excerpt field="ec_description"></atomic-product-excerpt>
      </atomic-product-section-description>
    </template>
  </atomic-product-template>
</atomic-commerce-product-list>
```

### Common Use Cases
- **Product Pricing**: Display current price, sale prices, and original prices
- **Ratings/Scores**: Highlight overall rating or review scores
- **Availability**: Show stock status or availability information
- **Discount Information**: Emphasize percentage discounts or savings amounts

### Styling Guidelines
- Use large, bold typography to ensure the emphasized content stands out
- Consider using colors that align with your brand and convey the right message (e.g., green for savings, red for urgency)
- Ensure sufficient contrast for accessibility
- Keep content concise as this section is meant for quick scanning
